SPARK Summer Camps

Beaverton Public Schools Robotics
Register Today!

Spark Academy offers two weeklong STEM summer camps:



Session 1: AUGUST 5 - 9
Session 2: JULY 8-12

Incoming Grades: 4–8
Check-in: 9 - 9:15 AM Mon–Fri
Checkout: 2:45 - 3 PM Mon–Fri
Tuition: $75/week

Each Monday - Friday session runs from 9 AM to 3 PM, with lunch and game activity breaks. Students must provide their own lunches.

These camp sessions will be held at: 15050 SW Weir Rd, Beaverton, OR 97007.

Each weeklong session costs $65 per student. If your family qualifies for free or reduced lunch, please notify us before you register to receive a discount code.

Sessions include (but are not limited to) activities in:


  • Robotics
  • Build, program, and explore LEGO Mindstorm robots!
  • Operate and analyze complex, 120lb robots built by FIRST Robotics teams
  • Graphic Design
  • Create and customize your own t-shirt using GIMP software!
  • Design and make your own vinyl decals, stickers, and T-Shirts
  • Computer Hardware Anatomy
  • Explore the inner workings of a computer by taking one apart and learning about how it works, from motherboards to RAM sticks!
  • Cryptography
  • Learn how to use cypher wheels to write your own coded messages
  • Entry-Level Engineering Simulation Software
  • CAD (Computer Aided Design) Software
  • Circuit Simulation Software
