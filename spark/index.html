Incoming Grades 4-8 | Summer | Weeklong Sessions | Full Day



Welcome to Spark Academy, organized in partnership with Beaverton Public Schools Robotics. This summer, learn how to design your own video game, play with robots, discover the secrets of cryptography, and more! Our program incorporates STEAM learning (science, technology, engineering, arts, and math) into fun camp activities. In our weeklong sessions, students will explore technology, make new friends, and build skills that last long after summer.

Spark Academy camps are all about promoting and spreading awareness, knowledge, and interest in STEAM (Science Technology Engineering Art and Math). Our camps are run by experienced students and mentors from Beaverton Public Schools' FIRST Robotics teams, the Westview Wildcats and School of Science and Technology's Flying Hedgehogs.

All proceeds will go towards supporting Beaverton robotics education programs. And, as BPS (Beaverton Public Schools) Robotics is a 501(c)3 non-profit charity, part of the tuition charged may be eligible for a tax deduction. Please discuss this with your tax preparer or accountant.
